About

  • This is Wild Times, my Dungeon and Dragons campaign that I am running with some online friends.
  • The idea for this came from the idea of people being cursed with ‘wild magic’ and being prosecuted for that.
  • I spent a lot of time putting together the world before school, and I'm still working on it now.
  • We have had one play session so far.
  • Our party is made up of a paladin, a rouge, a bard, a barbarian and a druid.
